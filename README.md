Project Overview
Data analysis project focused on customer satisfaction, call trends, and representative performance metrics.

Key Metrics & Analysis
Customer Satisfaction Analysis: Evaluated satisfaction ratings across 1,000+ calls to identify factors contributing to low scores.
Call Volume Trends: Analyzed daily and monthly call volume to determine peak hours and seasonal staffing needs.
Representative Benchmarking: Ranked performance of representatives (R01–R05) based on call duration and resolution satisfaction.
Sales & Purchase Correlation: Linked call interactions to purchase amounts to determine the ROI of various customer segments.
Duration Bucketing: Categorized call lengths to analyze the relationship between call time and customer happiness.

Key Insights:
Hiring Optimization: Identified specific months (e.g., March and October) where call volume spikes, recommending a temporary increase in staffing.
Training Needs: Pinpointed representatives who required additional satisfaction training based on sub-4.0 average ratings.
Operational Efficiency: Discovered that calls between 1–2 hours had a higher correlation with positive ratings compared to shorter "time-waster" calls.
